OUR STORY

At Vicky’s Design Studio, our journey is deeply rooted in a family legacy of excellence in construction and civil work. It all began with Maruti Sonaji Maske, a visionary craftsman specializing in building repairs, terrace waterproofing, and intricate civil work. His expertise laid a strong foundation of trust and quality that continues to inspire us today.

Building upon this legacy, Vijay Maruti Maske envisioned expanding the family's expertise into turnkey projects, seamlessly blending functionality with aesthetics. Guided by years of experiential knowledge, he ventured into the world of interior design and founded Vicky’s Design Studio.
